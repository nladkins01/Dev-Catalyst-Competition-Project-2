The Great Sport of Athletic Diving!

"Im just a simple guy swimming in a sea of sharks." -Don Johnson

Diving was first introduced in the official programs of the Summer Olympic Games at the 1904 Games of St. Louis and has been an Olympic Sport ever since.

The first Olympic diving events were contested by men and consisted of a platform diving event and also a plunge for distance event, which heralded victorius the diver who could reach the farthest underwater while remaining motionless after a ground-level standign dive.
