Olympic Summer Sports

"Citius, Altius, Fortius" -Olympic Motto (Latin for "Faster, Higher, Stronger")

The Summer Olympic Games were first held in 1896 and is an international multi-sport event that is hosted by a different city every four years.

The Olympics have increased in scope from a 42-event competition with fewer than 250 male competitors from 14 nations in 1896 to 302 events with 10,768 competitors from 204 nations in 2012, although this website will focus on a few of the larger sport events that are held in the Summer Olympic Games.
