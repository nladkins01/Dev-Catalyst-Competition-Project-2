The Marvelous Sport of Tennis!

"If you don't practice, you don't deserve to win." -Andre Agassi

Tennis was part of the Summer Olympic Games program from the inaugural 1896 Summer Olympics, but was dropped after the 1924 Summer Olympics.

After two apperances as a demonstration sport in 1968 and 1984, it was then a full medal sport at the 1988 Summer Olympics and has been played at every edition of the Games ssince then.
